Save Our Species


From the first cave paintings of human-animal partnerships to modern day animal memes, human and animal lives have always been intertwined. While there are countless examples of mutualism between humans and animals, the balance is starting to tip. The International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN) conducts research and publishes the IUCN Red List of Threatened Species, which assesses the conservation status of species worldwide.

Medicine

Many advancements in human health are thanks to animals, from laboratory research to extraction of animal parts for their medicinal value.

Policy

Policy can help or hurt species populations, whether it’s protecting an animal from extinction or enabling the decline of an animal by destroying its habitat.

Culture

Animals are often exploited in the name of culture, including commercialization of animal parts and domesticated house pets.
